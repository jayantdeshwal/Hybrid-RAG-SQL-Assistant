Hospital Frequently Asked Questions

Appointments
Patients can book appointments through the reception desk or the online portal.
Follow-up consultations should ideally be booked with the same doctor.

Laboratory Reports
Routine laboratory reports are usually available within 24 hours.
Radiology reports may take between 24 and 48 hours depending on the scan type.

ICU Rules
ICU visits are restricted and require prior approval from the treating team.
Visitors must follow hygiene and safety instructions before entering the ICU.
