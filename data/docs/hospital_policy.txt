Hospital Policy Manual

Visitor Policy
General visitors are allowed between 10:00 AM and 7:00 PM.
Only two visitors are allowed per patient at one time in general wards.
Visitors are not allowed in the ICU except with special permission from the attending doctor.

Discharge Policy
Patients are discharged only after the attending physician signs the discharge summary.
The billing desk must confirm that all charges are cleared before final discharge.
Patients should collect discharge prescriptions and follow-up instructions before leaving the hospital.

Emergency Policy
Emergency cases are handled on priority regardless of prior appointment.
Patients arriving with life-threatening symptoms are triaged immediately by the emergency response team.
