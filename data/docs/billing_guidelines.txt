Hospital Billing Guidelines

Billing Process
All inpatient charges are consolidated at the billing desk before discharge.
Consultation fees, room charges, pharmacy charges, and laboratory charges are included in the final bill.

Insurance
Cashless insurance is available only for empaneled insurance providers.
Patients using insurance must submit identity proof and insurance approval documents.
Any non-covered charges must be paid directly by the patient.

Refunds
Refunds for excess advance payment are processed within 7 working days.
Refunds are issued only after verification by the accounts department.
